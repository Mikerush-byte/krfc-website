Our Partners

Strategic collaborations driving agricultural transformation across Kenya

Building Stronger Agriculture Together

KRFC works with government agencies, private companies, NGOs, and community organizations to create a comprehensive ecosystem supporting rural agricultural development. Our partnerships expand our reach, enhance our services, and create sustainable opportunities for farmers and graduates.

We believe that transformation happens when we work collaboratively across sectors, leveraging each partner's unique strengths and resources.

Why Partner With KRFC?

Clear benefits for our partner organizations

01

Expanded Reach

Access to KRFC's network of farmers, graduates, and communities across rural Kenya.

02

Implementation Capacity

Leverage KRFC's established training infrastructure, facilities, and skilled workforce.

03

Local Knowledge

Partnership Models

Different ways organizations can engage with KRFC

Program Co-Implementation

Joint design and delivery of educational or development programs, sharing costs and expertise.

Best for: Development organizations, NGOs, Government

Market Linkage

Formal agreements connecting KRFC farmers and graduates with buyers, processors, or suppliers.

Best for: Private companies, agricultural businesses

Knowledge Exchange

Collaborative research, curriculum development, and learning exchange between organizations.

Best for: Educational institutions, research organizations

Resource Provision

Donations of equipment, materials, or technical expertise supporting KRFC programs.

Best for: Corporate partners, equipment manufacturers

Financial Support

Grants, sponsorships, or project financing enabling KRFC to expand and improve services.

Best for: Foundations, development partners, donors

Advocacy & Policy

Joint advocacy for agricultural policy changes and sector-wide improvements.

Best for: Government, industry associations, policy organizations
