About Kenya Rural Food Centre

Kenya Rural Food Centre (KRFC) Ltd is an agricultural development organization operating under the Agricultural Industrial Technical Application Project (AITAP). We are a comprehensive agri-industrial mini-super mall dedicated to transforming rural agriculture through education, innovation, and sustainable market linkages.

Our integrated approach combines practical training, technology transfer, value addition support, and direct market connections to empower smallholder farmers and create sustainable rural livelihoods.
