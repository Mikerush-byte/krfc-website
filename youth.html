KENYA RURAL FOOD CENTER YOUTH COLLEGE

Kenya Rural Food Centre Youth college for agriculture focus on transforming farming into a modern, profitable, and sustainable career for young people, specifically addressing the aging farming population and youth unemployment. They offer hands-on, competence-based technical and vocational training (TVET) to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern agricultural technologies
